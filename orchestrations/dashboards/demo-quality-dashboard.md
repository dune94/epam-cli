Layout (Top down, left to right):
1. Header / Navigation: Top title area and nav links to other dashboards.
2. Summary Chips: Overall POC quality counters (passed/partial/failed/not tested).
3. Phase Coverage Table: Coverage totals by phase.
4. Linked Backlog Bugs Table: Stories/bugs linked for traceability.
5. POC-Tested Stories Table: Detailed tested story grid with status badges, phase, and evidence counts.
